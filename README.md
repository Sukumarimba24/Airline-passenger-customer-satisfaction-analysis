# Airline-passenger-customer-satisfaction-analysis
**Summary:**

The passenger satisfaction data shows how happy customers are with airline services. It includes details like service quality, seat comfort, food, delays, and overall experience. By studying this data, we can understand what makes passengers satisfied or dissatisfied. This helps airlines improve their services and customer experience.


**passenger satisfaction analysis **:

Shows the percentage of satisfied vs dissatisfied passengers.

Higher satisfaction rate means better service quality.

Main factors: seat comfort, staff service, cleanliness.

Flight delays strongly reduce satisfaction.

Food and in-flight services also impact ratings.

Improving weak areas can increase overall satisfaction rate.
